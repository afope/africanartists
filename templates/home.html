archiving african women & men makers for what?

this is a publicly sourced archive and collaborative project. we are archiving male and female african artists because there needs to exist a space where this information can easily be sourced. for the love of archiving and documentation, and filling voids with our spirit works, and making our own spaces. images cannot be uploaded on this site as yet but when the image Url is requested, copy the link of the image you'd like to use from the internet and paste in the field. Please treat this project with love. bless.
